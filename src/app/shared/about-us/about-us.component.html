Vision

Caerus SkillmaTic India Pvt Ltd., has developed comprehensive Skill Management plan with a Vision to

  • To empower the student community and professionals with Right Skill Management Tools to effectively and objectively manage their career progression
  • Reduce the ever widening gap between the talent availability and the Industry requirements by effectively producing requisite skilled professionals
  • To provide significant value to all the stakeholders of the SkillmaTic platform

Mission

Caerus SkillmaTic India Pvt Ltd through its intelligent SkillmaTic platform, provides various tools and services for

  • Students/professionals to objectively acquire Industry required skills and competencies
  • Skill Providers to design, develop and impart various Training Curriculum to suit the industry trends
  • Educational institutes to design and develop various Industry relevant programs outside of the curriculum and develop industry -ready professions
  • Collaboration with Industry Subject Matter Experts and Accreditation organizations to effectively impart necessary certified skills programs
